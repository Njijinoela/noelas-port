WHO ARE WE?

Ajani Centre for Rights and Care is a duly registered Community based Organization based in Kajiado, Kenya. This organization was conceived out of our passion to alleviate the pain and suffering of survivors of sexual and gender based violence.

Our vision is to raise empowered individuals through provision of holistic support to survivors of sexual and gender based violence and other vulnerable members of the society.

We acknowledge that significant strides have been made towards formulation of policy with regard to sexual and gender based violence. However, there remains a big gap in the enforcement and in the after care of these survivors.

In fulfilling our vision, we have several missions and projects tailored around 2 main objectives:
i. Providing legal support.
ii. Providing psychosocial support

LEGAL SUPPORT SERVICES

Ajani Centre for rights and care provides legal advice and representation for survivors of sexual and gender based violence on pro bono basis. This includes follow up with investigative agencies such as the police and court attendances.

We also run sensitization campaigns to create awareness on the rights of survivors and all the vulnerable in society. We conduct legal research and advocate for policy changes with regard to sexual and gender based violence. Further, we carry out trainings for stakeholders such as community leaders, paralegals, administrators of rescue centers, police officers and members of the public.

PSYCHOSOCIAL SUPPORT SERVICES

We have a robust counseling department with several active trained counselors who provide survivors with the much needed support that they need to get through the emotional trauma that comes with sexual and gender based violence.

The department offers both individual and group counseling sessions for the survivors. They also provide psycho-education, mentorship and peer counseling.
